The CoinsGang Weekend event will be held at the luxury 5-star turkish resort in Antalya, Belek.

Gloria Hotels & Resorts is situated in Belek, Antalya in the midst of pine forests and at the seaside along Acisu river, in a region which embodies all natural beauties of the Mediterranean.

Gloria Hotels & Resorts spanning an area of 2.122.000 m2 consists of Gloria Golf Resort, Gloria Serenity Resort, Gloria Verde Resort, Gloria Golf Club and Gloria Sports Arena; an international multisports training complex.

Safe Tourism Certificate

Your safety
is our top
priority

Gloria Hotels & Resorts is pleased to welcome its guests, with the Safe Tourism Certificate. The certification is granted within the scope of the health and hygiene criteria set by the Ministry of Culture and Tourism and the Ministry of Health.

With new arrangements and precautions added to the bedrock values of comfort, quality and satisfaction, Gloria Hotels&Resort look forward to providing you with a happy stay experience, with peace of mind, in the heart of nature.

The interior and common areas of the 205,000 m2 facility are rearranged in accordance with social distance and health rules, and disinfected with the ULV system.

At the entrance to the hotel and in the main restaurant, guests’ temperatures are measured with a thermal camera. Guests can spend their holidays with peace of mind and the quality and comfort of Gloria, and ozone-disinfected rooms.
